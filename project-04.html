Deadwood PROJECT

Marketing Analysis

This project was carried out as part of the Marketing Strategy course at MODART International and focused on the strategic analysis of Deadwood Studios, a fashion brand specializing in upcycled leather.

I contributed to the overall brand assessment through market analysis, competitive benchmarking, and strategic reflection on positioning, segmentation, and the marketing mix. My approach was grounded in the study of sustainable fashion trends, evolving consumer behaviors, and the growing importance of environmental credibility in premium fashion.

The project culminated in a strategic recommendation for international expansion, proposing the opening of a flagship store in Copenhagen. This work demonstrates my ability to connect sustainability, branding, and business performance within a premium fashion context.
