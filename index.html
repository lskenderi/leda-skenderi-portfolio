ABOUT

My expertise exists at the intersection of luxury strategy, marketing, sustainability, and cultural insight.

With a background in life sciences and advanced training in luxury goods and fashion industries, I bring a structured, analytical approach to brand development. My foundation in biology shaped the way I think: through systems, long-term impact, and evidence-based decision-making. Today, I apply that mindset to the evolving challenges of the luxury sector.

I am particularly interested in how consumer expectations are shifting toward transparency, material literacy, and cultural relevance. As sustainability moves from narrative to necessity, brands must rethink not only what they produce, but how they position, communicate, and operationalize responsibility.

My work explores how innovation, regulatory evolution, and behavioral economics influence desirability in fashion and luxury. I focus on translating complex sustainability concepts into strategic opportunities such as aligning brand identity, product development, and market positioning.

Across strategy, merchandising, and sustainability advisory roles, I have worked cross-functionally with marketing, legal, and design teams to integrate responsible practices into business decisions. I am driven by clarity, precision, and the belief that the future of luxury lies in systems that make excellence and responsibility inseparable.

I am building a career dedicated to shaping brands that are not only desirable, but structurally intelligent.
