ABOUT

My expertise exists at the intersection of luxury strategy, sustainability, and cultural insight.

With a background in life sciences and an MBA in luxury goods and fashion industries, I bring an analytical approach to problem solving, whether that is applied to brand or product development. My studies and professional experiences trained me in systems thinking and sustainable strategies for long-term impact. Today, I apply that mindset to the evolving challenges in the luxury sector.

I am particularly interested in how consumer expectations are shifting toward transparency, material literacy, and cultural relevance. As sustainability moves from narrative to necessity, brands must rethink not only what they produce, but how they position, communicate, and operationalize responsibility.

I am building a career dedicated to shaping brands that are not only desirable, but responsible and structurally intelligent.
