Chanel Capsule — Courchevel

Merchandising Strategy

This project was carried out as part of a strategic merchandising assignment and involved designing a complete merchandising plan for a Chanel capsule collection in a seasonal boutique in Courchevel. The objective was to translate the brand’s DNA—timeless elegance, iconic codes, and desirability—into a coherent and high-performing retail experience.

I worked across the entire project scope: analysis of Chanel’s brand DNA and codes, concept development (Celestial Bodies), customer storytelling (ski day, après-ski, evening), visual merchandising, customer journey design, inventory management, stock rotation, and KPI monitoring. The project also included an estimation of potential revenue, linking creativity, experience, and commercial performance.

This work demonstrates my ability to align creative vision, luxury standards, and business logic within a premium retail environment.
